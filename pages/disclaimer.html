Service Disclaimer

Seiser Systems provides computer building, diagnostics, repair, upgrade, software cleanup, low-voltage electronics troubleshooting, and custom technology consultation services.

Free initial phone consultations are intended to understand the problem, project, or request. They do not include unlimited remote troubleshooting, on-site service, detailed design work, or repair labor. Paid work begins only after the scope, estimate, and next steps are clear.

All estimates are based on the information available at the time of evaluation. Final pricing may change if the issue is different than initially described, if additional problems are discovered, if parts are unavailable, or if the customer requests additional work.

Diagnostics and repairs are performed with reasonable care, but no repair outcome can be guaranteed. Some devices may have hidden damage, failing parts, corrupted software, or prior issues that affect repairability.

Customers are responsible for backing up important files, passwords, licenses, software keys, photos, documents, and personal data before service. Seiser Systems is not responsible for data loss caused by pre-existing device failure, storage corruption, malware, failed updates, or other conditions outside reasonable control.

Electronics troubleshooting is limited to low-voltage DC devices and practical repair evaluation. High-voltage systems, mains AC wiring, unsafe devices, appliances, and repairs requiring licensed electrical work may be declined or referred elsewhere.

Parts may carry manufacturer warranties separate from Seiser Systems labor. Customer-supplied parts are not guaranteed by Seiser Systems.

Seiser Systems will not recommend parts or services that appear unnecessary. If a repair is not worth the cost, that will be communicated before unnecessary paid work begins.
