About

Founded by Tanner Seiser.

Seiser Systems was founded by Tanner Seiser, a biomedical engineering student and systems-focused builder with hands-on experience in custom PCs, troubleshooting, software, electronics, and practical problem solving.

The goal is simple: help people understand, repair, upgrade, and design technology systems without being oversold. Whether the job is a custom PC build, a repair, a malware cleanup, or an early idea for a custom system, the process starts with listening first.

Seiser Systems is built around honest recommendations, clear communication, and quality-first work. If something is not worth fixing, upgrading, or building, that will be said clearly before unnecessary money is spent.
