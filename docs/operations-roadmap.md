# Operations Roadmap

This document outlines the future Seiser Systems customer workflow. It is planning documentation only. Customer data, invoices, payment records, and private operations tooling should not be stored in this public website repository.

## Customer Workflow

1. New request received
2. Initial phone consultation
3. Job scoped
4. Estimate created
5. Customer approves estimate
6. Work scheduled
7. Work completed
8. Invoice created
9. Payment received
10. Review requested
11. Job closed

## Workflow Notes

- The first contact should clarify what the customer needs and whether the request is a good fit.
- The initial phone consultation is free, but it should not become unlimited remote troubleshooting, on-site service, design work, or repair labor.
- Paid work begins only after the scope, estimate, and next steps are clear.
- Estimates and invoices should be reviewed by Tanner before being sent.
- Job records, customer contact details, invoice history, and payment status belong in a future private operations repository or approved private tool, not in the public website repository.
