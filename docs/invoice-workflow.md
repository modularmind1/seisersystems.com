# Invoice Workflow

This document describes how Seiser Systems invoices should work in a future private operations system.

## Invoice Timing

- Invoice is created only after customer approval or completed work.
- Invoice should be based on the approved scope, completed work, parts used, and any approved changes.
- Invoice should not be automatically sent without review.
- Payment tracking should be manual at first.

## Required Invoice Fields

- Customer info
- Date
- Service performed
- Labor
- Parts
- Total
- Payment status
- Notes

## Review Rules

- Tanner should review every invoice before it is sent.
- Invoices should be clear enough that the customer understands what was performed and what they are paying for.
- Payment status should be updated manually until a reliable private operations system exists.
- Invoices, payment notes, and customer billing information must not be stored in the public website repository.

## Early Payment Tracking

Manual payment tracking is acceptable at the beginning. A future private tracker should record:

- Unpaid
- Partially paid
- Paid
- Refunded or adjusted, if applicable
