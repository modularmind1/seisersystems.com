# Customer Statuses

This document defines future customer and job statuses for Seiser Systems operations tracking.

## Status Definitions

- **New Request** - A new service request has been received and has not yet been reviewed.
- **Contacted** - Seiser Systems has replied to the customer or attempted contact.
- **Consultation Scheduled** - An initial phone consultation has been scheduled.
- **Estimate Drafted** - An estimate exists as a draft and has not been sent.
- **Estimate Sent** - The estimate has been sent to the customer for review.
- **Approved** - The customer has approved the estimate or next step.
- **Declined** - The customer declined the estimate, service, or next step.
- **Parts Ordered** - Required parts have been ordered for approved work.
- **In Progress** - Work has started.
- **Completed** - Work is complete, but invoicing or follow-up may remain.
- **Invoiced** - An invoice has been created and sent or prepared for payment.
- **Paid** - Payment has been received and recorded.
- **Review Requested** - The customer has been asked to leave a review after service.
- **Closed** - The job is complete, payment and follow-up are resolved, and no further action is needed.

## Status Tracking Notes

- Statuses should be tracked in a private system, not in the public website repository.
- Status changes should be simple and understandable.
- A job should not be marked paid until payment is actually received.
- A job should not be closed if follow-up, payment, or customer communication is still unresolved.
